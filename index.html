Why am I taking coding bootcamp?

I finished a bootcamp in 2015 for iOS, and I immediately went to work for a startup. Unfortunately, that startup had to restructure, and I was let go in 2021. After searching for multiple jobs in iOS, and a number of interviews, I realized that I had gaps in my knowledge as a developer. Gaps not just in iOS, but other programming languages as well. I am taking this class from Epicodus to not only fill those knowledge gaps, but to round myself out as a developer as well.
